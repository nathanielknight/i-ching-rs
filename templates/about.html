This program simulates casting the I Ching using the three coin method. It works by hashing the date and your query to seed a random number generator and simulating coin flips with it. Specifically, it hashes the input with SHA256 and uses a ChaCha12 PRNG to simulate the coins.

You can save the URL of your casting to come back to it later.
